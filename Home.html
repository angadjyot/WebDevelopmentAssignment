WELCOME TO THE IKEA FAMILY

Ikea aim's is to serve people and give them value!

IKEA store is an European Multinational group that designs and sells ready to assemble the furniture, kitchen appliances and home accessories. It is a hub of the Furniture House that was found in Sweden. Customers can explore the products and have a wide range of choices to choose from. As of June 2019, there are 433 IKEA stores operating in 52 countries.The company is responsible for approximately 1% of world commercial-product wood consumption, making it one of the largest users of wood in the retail sector.
